PLASTIC MASTERBATCH (CALCIUM CARBONATE FILLER AND COLOR MASTERBATCH)
CALCIUM CARBONATE (CACO3) FILLER MASTERBATCH
ADC PLASTIC provides variety of CACO3 Filler Masterbatch under the trademark CALMAST® with compatible blending formulas, meeting all customer requirements especially the stability of quality. This is a perfect solution to optimize production costs for applications such as PE shopping bags, garbage bags, agricultural films, PP non-woven fabric bags, PP woven packaging, injection molded plastic household products, etc.
PRODUCT DETAILS
WHITE MASTERBATCH (TITANIUM DIOXIDE MASTERBATCH)
White Masterbatch from ADC PLASTIC are supplied to the market under the trademark COLMAST®, there are produced in a separate closed environment at our factory. Thanks to the investment in high-tech laboratory with the most advanced and modern measuring and testing equipment and the experience of leading experts, combined with the advantage of high-quality TiO2 raw materials resource, ADC PLASTIC can meet the maximum needs of customers, ensuring the highest cooperation benefits.
