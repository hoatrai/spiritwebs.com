ABIMEX GROUP

Abimex Group is a leading supplier of tapioca/cassava starch and other agricultural products.

Our factory local is located in Tay Ninh Province and has a monthly production capacity of 18,000 tons. Up to now, Abimex Group is the leading supplier of Tapioca Starch, Fish maw and other products.

With over 10 years of experience, professional staff, and an international sales team, we are confident in providing the best services quickly. Our motivation is to deliver on time while also offering competitive prices and high-quality products.

Along with the development, Abimex Group has been creating some new products from tapioca starch to meet the market’s demand. First of all, Abimex Group has developed and launched a premium product for kitties. It is made from 100% Natural Tapioca/Cassava starch (food grade). We hope that we can provide other new natural solutions for pet cares.

Welcome our partners from over the world to visit our office and factory!
