ABOUT US

MY DONG MECASTCO - A trusted manufacturer and foundry of cast iron and steel products in Hai Phong, Vietnam

MY DONG MECHANICS CASTING JOINT STOCK COMPANY was officially registered with the Hai Phong Department of Planning and Investment on January 15, 2008, and underwent its fifth amendment on October 7, 2025. The company was established following the restructuring of its predecessor, Truong Thanh Mechanical Casting Co., Ltd. Inheriting a solid technical foundation and a team of highly skilled engineers, MY DONG MECASTCO has quickly affirmed its position as a leading enterprise in Vietnam, specializing in the manufacturing and machining of cast iron and steel mechanical products.
☞ Main products: Cast iron products for drainage systems, valve boxes and covers, manhole covers, machinery components, and all custom cast iron products.
☞ Certifications and awards:
✪ Certifications: ISO 9001:2000 Quality Management System (2024), TUV Nord CERT Certificate (Federal Republic of Germany)
✪ Awards: Second-Class Labour Order (2003), Gold Medal and Certificate for High-Quality Vietnamese Goods (2002), among others.
✪ Markets: USA, Germany, Canada, Denmark, South Korea, Taiwan, etc.
✪ Commitment: High-quality products - Nationwide delivery.

My Dong continuously fosters innovation and develops new products to meet the evolving demands of customers.
