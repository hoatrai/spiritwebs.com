About Us

JIANGYIN BAIRUIJIA PLASTICS SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY CO., LTD

Our company was established in 2006 and has been specializing in the production of Polyolefin shrink film for 17 years. We are located in Huashi town, Jiangyin city, Jiangsu Province, which is known for its convenient transportation and advantageous geographical position.


We have 8 advanced polyolefin shrink film blowing machines, 5 slitting machines, 8 folding machines, 2 printing machine with a maximum of 10 colors, 3 machines for micro-perforated polyolefin shrink film, and 6 machines for polyolefin shrink film bags. The thickness of the film we can produce ranges from 10 microns to 38 microns, and our annual output is approximately 20000 tons.
