Products & Services

Granite Slabs & Tiles

Basalt Stone

We also supply Basalt Stone ( or also called Bazan Stone) in variety sizing and surface finishing.

Decorative Granite

Construction Services

Why us?

30 Years Experience

Being the first company to acquired the quarrying and processing granite stone license by Binh Dinh People’s Committee since 1978, we have had more than enough experience in this granite market.

Certified Experience

Each and every stone are tested with the highest standards of the industry: specifications such as polishing, water saturated absorption, compressive strength and softening coefficient are checked multiple times to ensure products’ quality.

People

We understand that creativity, skill and passion are the elements that will make our products different than others. Therefore, we ensure that we have the best people with the highest level of expertise at each step from exploring to product delivery.

Competitive Pricing

We in control of our quarries and factories, this provides us the ability to monitor effectively our production cost, thus bringing you the best competitive pricing on the market.

We got the tools

All of our quarries and factories are equipped with latest machinery, that can keep up the capacity of 150,000 m2 annually.

Product Guarantee

Our Granite products are made to last with time, in any conditions.
