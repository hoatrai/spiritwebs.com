Manufacturer & Supplier of Minerals, Tiles & Granite

Aryan Tiles

One of the trusted exporter and supplier of minerals, tiles & granite in India. An ISO 9001 : 2015 Certified Company, Aryan Tiles was founded in 2008 as a supplier of Quartz and Feldspar to Organised and Unorganised Ceramic Industry. It ventured into this sector guided by it's vision to provide a quality product at prices which make the end-consumer product (i.e., Ceramics) aesthetic and affordable.
