ABOUT US

AM Industries Vietnam is an independent company supplying metal components, steel structure, metal casting, metal fabrication, steel spare parts, industrial services, and procurement from Vietnam and Asia to Worldwide.

We have experience in fabricating and controlling production quality complied with Australian Standards, American Standards, and EU Standards. We also can apply other Standards and Sourcing platforms with High-Quality Control.

Our competitive advantage is a continued focus on improving processes, manufacturing, and services.

By managing the entire process, we control costs, stock and quality to consistently deliver projects on time and budget.
