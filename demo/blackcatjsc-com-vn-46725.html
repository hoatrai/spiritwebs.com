    Company Overview

    BLACK CAT INSULATION TECHNICAL JOINT STOCK COMPANY (STOCK CODE: BMK)
    Black Cat ®JSC is a public company that, after years of operation in the market, has risen to become one of the leading providers of Insulation, Scaffolding, Blasting and Painting, Passive Fire Protection and Refractory services in Vietnam. Our sustainable growth is carefully managed and maintained through the delivery of safe, high-quality services, efficient management, and the trust and appreciation of our customers for our products and services..
