Welcome to BongTiles

With factory based in the central of Viet Nam, BongTiles specializes in production and supply variety of handmade and semi-handmade products, including: handmade encaustic cement tiles, authentic big size terrazzo tiles, brass insert cement tiles, brass insert terrazzo tiles, glazed handmade ceramic tiles, terrazzo sinks and other customized concrete and clay-based products. The highly controlled production procedure and our skillful artisans make our products absolutely unique and at high quality. Each BongTile product is the combination of classic style and modern design, wonderfully durable, friendly, colorful and perfect fit to any project of your life.

LARGER TERRAZZO COLLECTION
