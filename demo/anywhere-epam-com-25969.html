  • Implement monitoring and observability with ELK
  • Manage secure data transfers and batch scheduling with SFTP and Autosys
  • Collaborate on Oracle DB performance tuning
  • Provide Level 2/3 support and root cause analysis during parallel runs
requirements

  • Experience in DevOps or SRE, preferably in banking, fintech or payments
  • Proven skills in automated testing and data validation
  • Hands-on with IBM MQ administration and message routing
  • Proficient in OpenShift, OpenStack and Ansible
  • Strong Linux administration and Pacemaker experience
  • Skilled in GitLab CI/CD and scripting with Bash or Python
  • No visa sponsorship available
nice to have
  • Familiarity with SWIFT messaging standards like ISO 20022 and payment clearing workflows
