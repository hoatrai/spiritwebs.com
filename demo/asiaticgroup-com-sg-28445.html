The Singapore 1000 Family of Rankings (Singapore 1000, Singapore SME 1000, Singapore International 100 and Fastest Growing 50) is the prestigious guide that ranks the nation’s top companies annually for close to 3 decades.

It is committed to profiling the success of businesses based on financial indicators such as revenue, net profit, return on equity and overseas revenue. The rankings are shared publicly and distributed to government sectors, embassies, trade organisations, banks, universities and libraries.

KELOX Multi-layer System. Pipe for radiator systems, hot and cold water systems and industrial applications.

The KELOX pipe is a 5-layer halogen-free plastic composite pipe which is impermeable to oxygen and water vapour. The inside and outside layers are made of cross-linked polyethylene (PEX). Themetal layer is made of aluminium with an overlapping weld along the length of the pipe. The bonding agent both inside and outside the aluminium guarantee that individual layers from a strong composite.

The unique feature of the PROtec fitting is its ability to hold and seal the joint in the inside of the pipe. The fitting, which has both a slim and short design, contains a yellow PROtector ring, which protects the sealing elements from damage.

The transparent sleeve is both functional and aesthetically pleasing. The sleeve was designed in this way so that no window is required to check whether the pipe has been joined correctly. More on KELOX
