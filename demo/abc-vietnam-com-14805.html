Introducing ABC Vietnam

Welcome to ABC Vietnam Manufacturing & Sourcing Co., Ltd – your trusted partner for high-quality manufacturing solutions in Vietnam. As a leading foreign-owned enterprise, we specialize in sheet metal fabrication, aluminum extrusion, die casting, and metal stamping, delivering precision and reliability for global industries. We provide more than just products – we offer solutions that drive success. From custom enclosures to complex assemblies, our advanced technology, skilled engineering, and strict quality control ensure every product exceeds expectations.

Beyond manufacturing, we offer end-to-end support, including sourcing, logistics, and quality assurance, to streamline your supply chain and reduce costs. Serving industries like electronics, renewable energy, and automotive, we are your gateway to seamless production relocation or outsourcing in Vietnam. Backed by a bilingual team fluent in English and Vietnamese, we bridge gaps to ensure smooth collaboration. Contact ABC Vietnam today, and let’s turn your vision into reality!
