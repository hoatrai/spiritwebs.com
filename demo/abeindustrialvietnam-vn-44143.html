    ABE Industrial Vietnam Co., Ltd.
    OUR COMPANY

    Established in Dec. 2006, ABE INDUSTRIAL VIETNAM CO., LTD is a subsidiary of ABE SEISAKUSHO CO. LTD, in Japan.

    We started the business as a PRESS PROCESSING company as OEM of our parent company in Vietnam. Our main products is for construction business, like Clips, Pipe support hangers and Pipe brackets, Angle frame, bracket, Air conditioner bracket, Electric ruck etc..

    In 2017, we decided to expanded our business not only the STAMPING, but also the SHEET METAL PROCESSING, and our target market is from Japanese to US, EU and South East Asia  utilizing graphic advantage & EPE(Export Processing Enterprise) license.

    Now a days, we received many business inquiry from various business area and countries outside Vietnam.

    Quality
    ISO CERTIFICATE

    ABE Industrial Vietnam was certified ISO 9001 in 2018 by the Vietnam Certification and Inspection Joint Stock Company (VQA). This certification is one of the most important certifications in the field of quality management and is recognized worldwide. ISO 9001 is an international standard for quality management developed by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO). This standard sets requirements for an organization’s quality management system and helps organizations ensure that their products and services meet customer requirements and relevant legal regulations.

    ABE Industrial Vietnam has achieved ISO 9001 certification after meeting the requirements of this standard. These requirements include designing, developing, manufacturing, and supplying products and services. The company had to ensure that their processes met the requirements of ISO 9001 and were audited by an independent auditing organization.

    The ISO 9001 certification demonstrates that ABE Industrial Vietnam has achieved a high standard in managing the quality of its products and services. This will help the company increase customer confidence and enhance competitiveness in the market.
