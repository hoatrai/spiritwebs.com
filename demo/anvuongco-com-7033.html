ABOUT US

Was established in 2021, headquartered in Ha Noi City by team with many years of experience in these field of distributing spare parts, electrical equipment, materials, etc for oil and gas.

At the beginning, ANVUONGCO mainly provided genuine industrial such as Encoder, inverter, PLC, HMI, Servo Motors, Gear Motors, all kinds of sensors, flow, temperature, pressure measurement devices, Valves, Solenoid Valves, etc.

From 2023, ANVUONGCO expanded its business into chemical products starting with industrial cleaning products & service as well as completed a range of engineering products and chemicals.

Besides the diversification of products and services, ANVUONGCO is committed to bringing dedication and enthusiasm into each product and service to provide the most comprehensive and professional care for our customers.
