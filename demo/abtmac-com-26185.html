About Us

Hebei Ablazebird Tech Co., Ltd is a professional company that specializes in providing solutions for thermally modified timber, wood vacuum drying, board joining, wood bending, and wood frame joining. Our team boasts extensive experience in thermal modification kilns, vacuum drying, and high-frequency woodworking machines. We are dedicated to recommending the best options tailored to your specific requirements.
