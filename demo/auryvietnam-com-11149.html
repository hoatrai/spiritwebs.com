06 REASONS TO CHOOSE AURY

#1

AN EXCELLENT CHOICE FOR HIGH-QUALITY REAL LEATHER

We select the best quality cowhide from Italia, EU, North America, South America. Our factory partners then process it into the most beautiful and durable finished leather.
#2

COMPETITIVE PRICING

Thanks to our import volume, our suppliers' speed of processing raw hides, and our expertise in handling import-export procedures, we are able to offer very affordable prices and reasonable minimum order quantities.
#3

ONE-ON-ONE CUSTOMER SERVICE

From listening to your needs to consulting and bringing your leather projects into reality, we are driven by our desire to satisfy our customers.
#4

EXPERTISE IN PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT

We are a versatile solution provider. We are able to supply, style, and customize leather for all purposes and design ideas. We take pride in our customers' exquisite final products.
#5

ADVANCED TECHNOLOGY

Our factories are equipped with cutting-edge machinery and leading technologies to process leather to the highest standards.
#6

A FAST AND RELIABLE PARTNER

With many years of experience in importing leather from major markets around the world and providing solutions to a wide range of customers, we know how to handle all phases of the purchasing process, from quote to final delivery, in a fast and efficient manner.
