    ABOUT US

    Cao Duong Co. Ltd. based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam and in operation since 2001 is a MEP contractor specializing in industrial engineering, manufacturing and construction in Vietnam and throughout the Asia/Southeast Asia regions.

    Since our founding, we have designed and delivered high quality industrial products for high profile clients such as Heineken, Carlsberg, Pepsico, Coca-Cola and Vinamilk to name a few and who are often repeat customers.

    At Cao Duong,the focus is on high quality and meeting our customer’s expectations. We achieve this quality through investment in our personnel and conforming to international standards all the while, maintaining positive collaboration at the senior levels of management.

    Cao Duong is at the front of modern technology through their years of experience to deliver quality products and services that will last for many years.
