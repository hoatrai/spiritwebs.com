Everything we do is made to inspire and change this beautiful world!

Our bamboo products such as bamboo sticks, bamboo mats, bamboo screens and many other premium bamboo furniture for construction come from our plantations in/ Central Vietnam, then sustainably transformed in our facilities, ensuring top quality and fair, affordable market prices.

The best bamboo for sale, boasting superior durability and optimal diameter and length, is certified by our four autonomous regional corporations. Because we manage our own forests, the bamboo undergoes a meticulous screening process in the bamboo forest, focusing on diameter uniformity and ensuring that each piece is free of cracks to ensure long-term durability. Every year, we closely monitor all operations, ensuring compliance with approved harvest volumes, tree maturity and ecological location of the harvest site, among other criteria. We are proud to be the only bamboo exporter in Vietnam with a certificate of origin, ensuring environmental awareness of our bamboo origins, highlighting sustainable practices and ensuring approved commercial harvesting.

Introducing Central Bamboo

Established in 2000, we started as a family farm, mainly serving trading companies and suppliers in Vietnam. The official merger of Central Bamboo into EcoQuest in 2024 is a strategic stepping stone, creating a superior advantage: paving the way for export activities without any intermediaries, bringing high-quality bamboo products directly to the global market.” Business registration certificate No. 4001285235 from the Department of Planning and Investment.

This milestone marks a turning point, allowing us to expand our reach by exporting directly to international markets.

Our location in Da Nang city, central Vietnam is a significant advantage.

Known as a hub for bamboo crafts, the region provides us with an abundant supply of high-quality bamboo and access to some of the most skilled artisans in the country. These local advantages are crucial in allowing us to steadily expand our production capacity. Combining high-quality specialty bamboo raw materials, proximity to a strategic seaport and the ability to export directly without intermediaries after the EcoQuest merger (2024) creates a breakthrough for Central Bamboo

 

Many years of experience in the business field

With over 30 years of experience, we have become experts in every aspect of the craft — from manufacturing techniques and quality control to product design. This deep expertise allows us to create truly unique and attractive handicrafts that meet the high standards of our export markets.
