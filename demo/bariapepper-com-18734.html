BARIA PEPPER
BARIA PEPPER was established in 1985 with the aim to take considerable knowledge of both the product and the local market and use our experience to reach more customers domestically and internationally. Today we have the trust and respect of our clients, with we value highly.

In 2009 we first began exporting pepper to the worldwide market with our brand name BARIA PEPPER. This brand name derives from our location in Ba Ria province, where the city of Vung Tau. Here is where we make our home and enjoy the great favor of nature, which gives us rich red soil for the growing of Pepper, Cacao and Cashew nuts,...

BARIA PEPPER is about Pepper - Black Pepper, White Pepper, Green Pepper, Powdered Pepper - one of the most popular spices in the world over the past 3000 years. We specialize in selection, trading, importing and exporting all types of pepper products.

BARIA PEPPER products have been exported to the Americas, Europe, the Midle-East and other countries in Asia.

We has grown pepers for years. In the midst of the Baria Pepper growing regions, we maintain a close connection with the local farmers. This gives us a great advantage in controlling quaility and price, purchasing the best possible product at the best possible haverst time, shipping it quickly to our customers.

For further information, don't hesitate to contact us. Hope to establish business with you for long time.
