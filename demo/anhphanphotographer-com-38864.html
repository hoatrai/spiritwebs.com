NICE TO MEET YOU HERE !

HELLO GUYS !

I’m Anh Phan – vietnam wedding photographer based in Hoi An , Da Nang and anywhere for destination wedding. When I first picked up the camera, I knew I only wanted to capture the purest and happiest moments of life. Fortunately, I am able to follow that passion and given the privilege to photograph wedded couples all around the globe. As I look through the camera lenses, I don’t only aim at people, at the concept, at the colour scheme, but I also and foremost aim for the love, the vibe and the intangible chemistry between two people being crazily in love. But I don’t want to be the only one who sees it, I want to visualise it so everyone can see and cherish the love with me, so I choose photography as my very own medium.
