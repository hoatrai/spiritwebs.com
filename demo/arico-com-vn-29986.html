WHO WE ARE

ARICO, a member of the HOSHIZAKI Group (Japan), traces its origins back to 1977, beginning as a mechanical workshop in Phu Lam, Ho Chi Minh City. The organization has undergone multiple stages of development, evolving from a state-owned “Refrigeration Industrial Company” operation and completing equitization in 1999. In 2007, the Asia Refrigeration Industry entity (ARICO) was officially established to implement the relocation of production facilities out of residential areas and to mobilize investment resources for the development of its manufacturing plant at Tan Tao Industrial Park.

Over the years, ARICO has built a strong reputation as a pioneer and trusted partner in delivering and applying refrigeration engineering solutions for food processing, freezing, and cold storage in Vietnam. ARICO’s customer base extends nationwide from South to North and has expanded into international markets such as India and Oceania. Since joining the HOSHIZAKI Group in March 2025, ARICO has entered a new phase of growth, strengthening its international integration and enhancing its capabilities to serve customers in the F&B sector.

ARICO believes that continuous improvement and innovation, combined with a customer-centric mindset, are essential to delivering reliability and long-term value while sustaining competitive advantage in a dynamic business environment. All activities at ARICO are guided by its core values: Agility – Reliability – Innovation – Conformity – One Team.

Our moto: “Always in favour of your development”
