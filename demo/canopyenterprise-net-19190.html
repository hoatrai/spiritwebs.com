    Welcome to Our Website!

    Welcome to Canopy Enterprise, where excellence meets innovation in the world of chemical products. we are dedicated to providing top-tier chemical solutions to industries across the globe.

    Canopy Enterprise has a global market presence as a Manufacturer, Exporter and Supplier of Chemicals based in Ankleshwar, Gujarat, India. Our range of chemicals includes Hydrochloric Acid, Nitric Acid, Caustic Flakes,Caustic Lye, Aluminium Sulphate, Sodium Acetate, Ferrous sulphate, Industrial Dyes, Color Pigments, Calcium Hypochlorite, Sulphuric Acid 98%, Acetic Acid, Hydrogen Peroxide, Ferric Chloride, Precipitated Silica, and many more. Our products are commonly used in industries such as plastic, printing ink, leather, and more. We have garnered a huge clientele with the help of high-quality products, a competent team, and sophisticated infrastructure.

    With a strong foundation in quality and customer satisfaction, we specialize in exporting a diverse array of chemicals that cater to various industrial needs. Our commitment to excellence is further reinforced by our association with Shardin Intermediates, a state-of-the art manufacturing unit in Dahej, India, producing specialty chemicals, fragrance-related products, and pharma intermediates, as well as Sterk Healthcare.
