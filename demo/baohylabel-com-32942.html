About BaoHy



Welcome to BaoHy Label's website!

We are a Vietnam-based label manufacturer and specialize in:

  • Woven edged labels
  • Printed labels
  • Stylish woven ribbons

BaoHy is located in Saigon/Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. With its current scale and capability, the company takes pride in being one of the largest private woven label manufacturers serving the clothing and leather industries in Vietnam.


Although BaoHy manufactures different types of labels, the company bears in mind that its scope of business is far beyond making labels. Its business is to ensure and enhance your quality clothing and leather products and to help differentiate your products in the market.



   



Our operation is not limited by geography; the company is serving customers around the country of Vietnam and is expanding its markets internationally. Please contact us for further information and for all of your labeling inquiries.
