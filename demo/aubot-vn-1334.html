CORE SOLUTION SUITE

AUBOT delivers an integrated suite of automation technologies designed to optimize every stage of cargo flow. From high-performance AGVs and AS/RS systems to intelligent software platforms that orchestrate operations in real time, our solutions work seamlessly together to move, store, and manage goods with precision. Built for scalability and engineered to global standards, AUBOT empowers businesses to achieve higher throughput, greater reliability, and a smarter, greener operational future.

AUBOT’s automated warehouse solutions integrate advanced robotics and intelligent software to maximize storage density and ensure 24/7 operational continuity. By replacing manual processes with precision automation, we eliminate bottlenecks and guarantee unmatched accuracy for logistics centers and cold-chain operations. Our “Made in Vietnam” technology delivers global-standard efficiency with an optimized total cost of ownership, providing businesses a sustainable and scalable path forward.

 

AUBOT’s automated material handling solutions integrate specialized AGVs and a smart Fleet Management System (FMS) to redefine factory shop floor logistics. By replacing manual processes with 24/7 robotic transport, we eliminate bottlenecks and safety risks while ensuring the precise, damage-free movement of heavy loads. This synchronized “digital brain” provides real-time tracking and intelligent traffic coordination to maximize throughput and operational efficiency.

AUBOT solves modern urban parking challenges by transforming traditional garages into high-performance robotic systems powered by industrial automation technology. Our solution utilizes smart control software and specialized AGVs to ensure fast vehicle retrieval, maximum space efficiency, and absolute safety. Designed for the global market, we deliver a premium, touch-free parking experience that optimizes both land use and operational flow.

Our software platform unifies every layer of warehouse and logistics automation through intelligent, real-time control. With integrated WMS, WCS, fleet management, and data analytics, the system orchestrates goods, equipment, and workflows with precision—enabling end-to-end visibility, faster decision-making, and consistently optimized cargo flow across your operations.
