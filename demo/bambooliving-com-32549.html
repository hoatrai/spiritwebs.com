WELCOME TO BAMBOO LIVING

WE ARE A FULL-SERVICE ARCHITECTURE FIRM CREATING THE WORLD'S ONLY INTERNATIONALLY CERTIFIED, PERMIT-READY, PREFABRICATED BAMBOO BUILDINGS AND HOME FURNISHINGS.

Our international team of architects and designers seamlessly blends time-honored bamboo craftsmanship with modern design and fabrication techniques, creating custom bamboo homes that are truly one-of-a-kind. Since 1995, we've proudly crafted hundreds of stunning bamboo homes and buildings for clients around the globe. Explore our work and let us help you build your next project!

DESIGN

STRENGTH, VALUE, BEAUTY

WE BUILD BEAUTIFUL HOMES THAT ARE LOW-MAINTENANCE AND DESIGNED TO LAST A LIFETIME

TERMITE & PEST RESISTANT

Naturally pest-resistant, our bamboo is also pressure-treated with non-toxic borates, creating a termite and pest resistant environment. From sourcing to assembly, every step of our process is independently inspected and verified to meet international building codes, ensuring long-lasting safety.

WORLDWIDE DELIVERY

Each Bamboo Living home is built to order and tailored to fit a wide range of locations. With our advanced prefabrication methods and the flexibility to customize buildings for any climate, our homes and structures can be shipped and assembled almost anywhere in the world.

SUPERIOR PERFORMANCE

Bamboo is exceptionally strong, offering the tensile strength of mild steel and the compression strength of concrete. Our bamboo buildings have proven their resilience, withstanding 200 mph hurricane winds and a powerful 6.9 earthquake.

INTERNATIONAL STANDARDS

Bamboo Living homes are the first and only Internationally Code Certified bamboo structures in the world. Build legally and with confidence, no matter where in the world your new home will be.

ENVIRONMENTAL STEWARDSHIP

OUR BUILDINGS WORK IN HARMONY WITH THE NATURE THAT SURROUNDS THEM.

At Bamboo Living, we believe in building a greener future. By using bamboo instead of traditional wood, we embrace sustainable practices that help preserve forests and natural resources. Our mission is to maximize material efficiency while minimizing our impact on both the environment and human health.

Our bamboo structures use fewer non-renewable materials, produce less waste, and conserve energy by prioritizing passive cooling, ventilation, and natural light. We're proud to be leading the way in green building and invite you to join the Bamboo Living family. Together, let's take sustainable building to new heights and create a future that thrives in harmony with nature.
