ATAD Steel Structure Corporation

WHO WE ARE

ATAD Steel Structure Corporation is the leading Steel Structure Company in Southeast Asia. ATAD specializes in providing complete solutions including: Design, Fabrication and Erection of high-quality steel structure products. With a well-trained workforce of over 2,700 employees and three advanced factories, we have constructed more than 4,000 buildings worldwide and have 9 companies & representative offices across Asia-Pacific. ATAD Long An factory

ISO 9001:2008, ISO 14001:2004, OHSAS 18001:2007

• Acquired ISO 9001 certification - an international standard quality management system, helping the Company improve quality, reputation
• Acquired ISO14001 certification to assess the performance of ATAD’s Environmental Management System
• Continued to achieve OHSAS 18001 - the international standard for occupational health and safety management systems

2013

Vietnam Gold Star Award

Was rewarded Vietnam Gold Star Award which honored the enterprises that had achieved good business performance and sustainable development and undertaken good social responsibility. Established ATAD Myanmar Co., ltd.

2015

Officially changed name to “ATAD Steel Structure Corporation”

Achieved CE Marking which proved that ATAD products complied with the essential requirements of European health, safety and environmental protection legislation. Opened ATAD Thailand legal entities and Cambodia representative offices

2016

VNR500

First time in Top 500 largest private enterprises in Vietnam. This was a recognition and an objective assessment of the business results that ATAD achieved

2017

Inauguration of ATAD Dong Nai

• Inauguration of ATAD Dong Nai - the most modern and largest steel structure factory in the region, the first LEED Gold steel structure factory in Asia and the first LEED Platinum factory office in Vietnam
• Continued to be honored in Top 500 largest private enterprises in Vietnam

2018

New logo for new stage

New logo for new stage, which helps enhance ATAD’s image and suits with company development orientation in the future. Continued to be honored in the TOP 500 Largest Private Enterprises In Vietnam (VNR500) and TOP 50 Vietnam Best growth (FAST500).
