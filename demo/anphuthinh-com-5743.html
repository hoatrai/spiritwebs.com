INTRODUCE

AN PHU THINH

An Phu Thinh Joint Stock Company is a leading enterprise in Vietnam, specializing in the production of industrial gloves and personal protective equipment. We integrate the entire production process at a single 2.5-hectare facility.

The company was founded with the goal of providing high-quality and safe products to consumers. Over time, we have expanded our scale and improved our production technology.
